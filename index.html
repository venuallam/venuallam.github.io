PEC - Picture Exchange Communication

Empowering children with autism through visual communication.

About PEC

PEC is an assistive communication application designed specifically for children and individuals with communication difficulties, including those on the autism spectrum. The app enables users to communicate their needs, feelings, and responses through an intuitive, visual-first interface using customizable communication cards.

Core Functionality

  • Visual Communication: Create and organize communication cards with images and labels.
  • Text-to-Speech: High-quality audio feedback when a card is selected to reinforce vocal communication.
  • Customizable Library: Add your own photos via camera or gallery to personalize the learning experience.
  • Secure Backup: Keep your personalized library safe across devices with optional Google Drive sync.

Transparency & Data Usage

How We Use Your Data

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. PEC is designed as an offline-first tool. All images and category data you create are stored locally on your device.

If you choose to enable the Google Drive Sync feature, the app will request access to a private, restricted folder on your Google Drive called the "Application Data folder." This access is used strictly to upload and download encrypted backups of your communication cards. We cannot access, see, or modify any other files in your Google Drive.
