Privacy Policy for PEC (Picture Exchange Communication)

Last updated: May 15, 2026

PEC ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information when you use it.

1. Information Collection and Use

The PEC app is designed to function primarily as an offline tool. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information to external servers. All data you create, including categories and images, is stored locally on your device unless you explicitly enable the optional cloud backup feature.

Permissions Used:

  • Camera: We request access to your device's camera to allow you to take photos of objects or actions to create custom communication cards. Photos taken are stored only in the app's internal storage on your device.
  • Storage (Images/Media): We request access to your device's gallery or media storage to allow you to select existing photos to use as communication cards. The app only accesses files that you specifically select.

2. Data Storage and Backup

All data created within the app (category names, image labels, and the images themselves) is stored in the app's private internal storage.

  • Local Storage: By default, your data stays on your device.
  • Optional Google Drive Sync: You may choose to enable Google Drive backup and sync. If enabled, the app will upload a compressed backup of your communication cards and settings to your personal Google Drive account.
  • Private App Data Folder: The app only requests access to its own private "Application Data" folder on your Google Drive. We cannot see, access, or modify any other files or folders in your Google Drive account.
  • Data Deletion: If you uninstall the app, local data is deleted. Backups on Google Drive will remain until you manually delete them or remove the app's access via your Google Account settings.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Glide: For efficient image loading and caching. Glide does not collect personal information through our implementation.
  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and use your device's Advertising ID to show personalized ads and for analytics purposes.
  • Google Drive API: Used optionally to provide backup and restore functionality. The app only interacts with its own restricted app data folder.

We do not use any other analytics or tracking software.

4. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for individuals with communication difficulties, including children. Because we do not collect any personal data (except for optional backups stored in the user's own Google account) or transmit information to our own servers, the app is safe for use by children in accordance with privacy regulations like COPPA.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing us access to your camera, storage, and optional cloud backup. Since all data stays on your device or within your private Google Drive folder, the security of your data depends on the security of your physical device and your Google account.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
